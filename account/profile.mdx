## Accessing your settings

Open your settings by clicking the gear icon in the sidebar, or navigate directly to `/settings`.

## Updating your profile

You can update your first name, last name, and GitHub handle from the **Profile Details** section.

<Note>
  Your email address is read-only and is managed through your authentication provider. You cannot change it from within Codexa.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the gear icon in the sidebar, or go to `/settings`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your details">
    Update any of the following fields:

    - **First Name** — your given name (up to 30 characters)
    - **Last Name** — your family name (up to 30 characters)
    - **GitHub Handle** — a link to your GitHub profile in the format `https://github.com/username`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save Changes**. A confirmation message will appear when your profile has been updated successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Danger zone

<Warning>
  Permanently remove your Account and all of its contents from the Codexa servers. This action is not reversible.
</Warning>

To delete your account, scroll to the **Danger Zone** section at the bottom of the Settings page and click **Delete Account**.

You will be asked to confirm before anything is deleted. Once confirmed, your account and all associated projects are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
