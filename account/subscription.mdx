## Available plans

Codexa offers two plans. All accounts start on the Free plan.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Free" icon="circle-user">
    Basic access to the Codexa editor. Create and manage projects without any cost. Suitable for personal use and exploration.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Paid" icon="sparkles">
    Everything in the Free plan, plus access to the AI assistant and enhanced features to accelerate your development workflow.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Your current plan is displayed in the dashboard sidebar below the Codexa logo.
</Note>

## Subscribing to the Paid plan

You can upgrade directly from the Codexa interface. The subscription flow is initiated in-app and walks you through the steps to activate your Paid plan.

## Billing

Payments are processed securely through [Paystack](https://paystack.com). Codexa does not store your payment details.

## Supported sign-in methods

You can create a Codexa account using either of the following methods:

- **Email and password**
- **Google**

Your sign-in method does not affect which plan you can access or how billing works.
