title: "Editor themes"
description: "Personalize your coding environment by choosing from 50+ built-in Monaco editor themes."
---

Codexa uses the [Monaco editor](https://microsoft.github.io/monaco-editor/) and ships with over 50 themes. Your theme preference is saved automatically so it persists across sessions and devices.

## Default theme

The default theme is **VS Dark** (`vs-dark`), Monaco's built-in dark theme.

## Changing your theme

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Settings menu">
    In the editor, click the **gear icon** (Settings) in the top-right area of the editor header bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Theme submenu">
    Hover over **Theme** in the dropdown menu. A scrollable list of all available themes appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a theme">
    Click any theme name to apply it immediately. The editor updates in real time. The active theme is shown in bold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your theme selection is saved automatically. The next time you open the editor, it will use the theme you last selected.
</Note>
