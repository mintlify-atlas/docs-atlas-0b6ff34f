---

The Settings page lets you update your profile information and manage your account.

## Opening settings

Navigate to `/settings` or click the gear icon in the sidebar.

## Updating your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the gear icon in the sidebar, or go to `/settings` directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your profile details">
    Update any of the following fields under **Profile Details**:

    - **First name**
    - **Last name**
    - **GitHub handle** — accepts a full profile URL (e.g., `https://github.com/username`)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save Changes**. The button shows a loading indicator while the update is in progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile fields

| Field | Editable | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| First name | Yes | — |
| Last name | Yes | — |
| GitHub handle | Yes | Enter your full GitHub profile URL |
| Email address | No | Managed through your authentication provider |

<Note>
  Your email address is read-only. It is managed securely through the authentication provider you used to sign up and cannot be changed from within Codexa.
</Note>

## Danger zone

<Warning>
  **Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.** All your projects and data will be removed from Codexa's servers immediately. There is no way to recover this data after deletion.

  To delete your account, scroll to the **Danger Zone** section on the Settings page and click **Delete Account**.
</Warning>
