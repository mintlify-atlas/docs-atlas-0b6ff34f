---
title: "Templates"
description: "Start a new project from one of 37 pre-configured language and framework templates."
---

The Templates page lets you jumpstart a project with a pre-configured language or framework. Navigate to it from the **sidebar** by clicking the **Templates** link.

---

## Starting from a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the template gallery">
    The Templates page displays all 37 available templates as cards, each showing the framework logo and name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click a template card">
    Clicking a card opens the **Create New Project** modal with the **Framework / Language** field pre-filled to match the template you selected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a project name">
    Type a name for your project in the **Project Name** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the project">
    Click **Create Project**. You are redirected to the editor for your new project immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---
