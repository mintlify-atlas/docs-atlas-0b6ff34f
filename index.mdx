Codexa is a browser-based code editor built for developers who want AI assistance directly in their workflow. Write, edit, and organize code with a familiar VS Code-like interface — powered by Monaco — while an AI assistant helps you build faster through natural conversation and real-time suggestions.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at Codexa with your email. Authentication is handled securely — no configuration needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    From the dashboard, click **New Project**, choose a name and language, and optionally pick a template to scaffold your file structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start coding with AI">
    Open your project in the editor. Use the **AI Assistant** panel on the right to ask questions or request changes, and let inline autocomplete suggest completions as you type.
  </Step>
</Steps>
