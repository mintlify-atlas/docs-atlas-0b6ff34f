---
title: "Authentication"
description: "How to sign up, log in, and manage your session in Codexa."
---

Codexa uses Google Sign-In for authentication. Your session is maintained automatically across visits.

## Sign up

Go to the Codexa login page and click **Sign in with Google**. Authenticate with your Google account — if no Codexa account exists for that identity, one is created automatically.

All new accounts start on the **free plan**, which includes up to 3 projects. You can upgrade to a **paid plan** to unlock unlimited projects and additional AI features.

## Log in

Click **Sign in with Google** on the login page. You are redirected through Google's OAuth flow and returned to your Codexa dashboard on success.

## Staying signed in

Codexa manages your session automatically. As long as your session is active, you stay logged in across page refreshes and new tabs. You do not need to log in again on each visit.

<Warning>
  If your session expires, Codexa redirects you to `/login` automatically. Any unsaved work in the editor may be lost. Save your files regularly while working.
</Warning>

## Signing out

You can sign out from two places:

- **Dashboard**: Click your name or avatar in the sidebar, then click **Log Out**.
- **Editor**: Click the profile picture in the top-right of the editor header, then click **Log Out**.
